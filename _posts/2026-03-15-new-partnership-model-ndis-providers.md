Our partnership model is straightforward: NDIS providers engage TrustCare Support as their dedicated clinical nursing partner for participants requiring complex or high intensity nursing care. We embed within your service delivery framework — attending participant homes under your service agreement, contributing to support plans, and providing competency assessment and training for your support workers.

This model means providers retain their participant relationships and case management responsibilities, while TrustCare handles the clinical nursing components that require AHPRA registration and specialist competency. We do not recruit participants away from partner providers and operate under strict confidentiality obligations.
