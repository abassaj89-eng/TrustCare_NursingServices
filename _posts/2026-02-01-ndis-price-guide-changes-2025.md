The NDIS pricing framework sets maximum hourly rates that registered providers may charge for supports. Key changes in the 2025 update include revised rates for High Intensity Daily Personal Activities, updated travel and non-face-to-face time provisions, and new line items for RN delegation activities. Providers must ensure billing aligns with the current Support Catalogue and that staff delivering supports are correctly classified under the relevant registration group.
