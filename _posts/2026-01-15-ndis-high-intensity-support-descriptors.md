The NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission has defined 10 High Intensity Daily Personal Activities (HIDPAs) that require specialist training and competency assessment. These include complex bowel care, enteral feeding, tracheostomy management, ventilator support, and more. Providers must ensure workers are trained and assessed by a qualified health professional — such as a Registered Nurse — before delivering these supports. TrustCare Support provides competency assessment and training for all 10 HIDPAs in Perth, WA.

For more information, visit the <a href="https://www.ndiscommission.gov.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener" style="color:var(--blue);">NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission</a>.
