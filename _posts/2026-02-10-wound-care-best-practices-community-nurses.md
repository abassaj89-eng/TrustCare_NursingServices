Wound assessment should follow the TIME framework — Tissue type, Infection or inflammation, Moisture balance, and Edge of wound — to guide clinical decision-making. Dressing selection must be based on wound type, exudate level, and healing phase. For NDIS participants, a current wound management plan signed by an AHPRA-registered Registered Nurse is essential for compliance and continuity of care.

Community nurses should document wound dimensions, photography (with consent), and treatment response at every visit. Where wounds are complex, chronic, or deteriorating, referral to a wound specialist or Nurse Practitioner should be considered.
