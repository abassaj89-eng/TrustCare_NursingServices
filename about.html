🤝
Independent RN Service — Not an NDIS Registered Provider

TrustCare Support provides independent Registered Nurse services exclusively to registered NDIS providers and support coordinators to fill clinical nursing gaps. We do not contract directly with participants — all engagements are arranged through a registered NDIS provider or plan manager.

Compliance & Standards

Our Commitment to AHPRA and NDIS Practice Standards

🏥

AHPRA-Registered Registered Nurse

All nursing care is delivered by an AHPRA-registered Registered Nurse — ensuring every clinical intervention meets the highest professional standard.

📋

NMBA Standards for Practice

Practice aligned with the Nursing and Midwifery Board of Australia (NMBA) Registered Nurse Standards for Practice (2016).

✅

NDIS Code of Conduct

Adherence to the NDIS Code of Conduct in every interaction with participants, providers, and families.

🔒

Defined Scope of Practice

All care is delivered within the defined scope of practice for a Registered Nurse, ensuring safety and regulatory compliance at all times.

Important: TrustCare Support is not an NDIS registered provider. We are an independent AHPRA-registered Registered Nurse service engaged by registered NDIS providers to fill clinical nursing gaps. We do not contract directly with participants. NDIS funding for TrustCare's services is billed through your registered provider framework, not directly through TrustCare. We adhere to the NDIS Code of Conduct, AHPRA registration requirements, and NMBA Standards for Practice in every engagement.
