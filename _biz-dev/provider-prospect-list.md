## How to Use This List

TrustCare's pitch to each provider should be tailored by tier:

- **Tier 1 — Primary Clinical Arm Pitch:** "We can be your in-house RN team for your complex participants. Zero recruitment overhead, NDIS-compliant from day one."
- **Tier 2 — Capacity and Overflow Pitch:** "We can supplement your existing clinical team — overflow shifts, HIDPA training for your support workers, or specific procedure delivery."
- **Tier 3 — Future Pipeline / Warm Introduction Pitch:** "As your participants age and become more complex, we are here. Let us introduce ourselves now."

**Billing note:** For SDA providers, remind them that NDIS community nursing care (Group 0114) is billed from the *participant's plan*, not the provider's operational budget. Engaging TrustCare is zero cost to the provider's income statement.
