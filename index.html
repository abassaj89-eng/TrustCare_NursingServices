Our Process

How We Support NDIS Providers and Support Coordinators

TrustCare is Perth's NDIS nursing subcontractor for registered providers. We do not deliver supports as a registered provider or contract directly with participants. Instead, registered NDIS providers engage us to deliver the clinical nursing component of a participant's plan — within their service framework, under their service agreement.

1

Referral Received

Referral received from a registered NDIS provider or support coordinator on behalf of a participant.

2

Clinical Triage and Review

Clinical triage and review by Registered Nurse within 24 hours of receiving the referral.

3

Engagement Under Service Agreement

Engagement formalised under an NDIS provider service agreement with the referring registered provider.

4

Delivery of Nursing Care

Delivery of nursing care within the provider framework, aligned with the participant's NDIS plan and care goals.

5

Ongoing Communication

Ongoing communication with GP, allied health, and care teams to ensure coordinated, safe, and holistic participant care.

Clinical Credibility

AHPRA-Regulated Registered Nurse

TrustCare is not an NDIS registered provider. All clinical nursing care is delivered by an AHPRA-registered Registered Nurse operating under AHPRA regulation and the NMBA Registered Nurse Standards for Practice (2016). You can verify our registration directly on the public AHPRA register.

NDIS Code of Conduct

Defined Scope of Practice
